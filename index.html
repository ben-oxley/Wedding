Gifts

A few people have asked us if we have a gift registry or what gifts we would like. We're really pleased that everyone can come and celebrate with us on the day and we're really grateful for that. If you would still like to get us something, and don't know what to get, we would really appreciate any contributions towards our honeymoon. There will be a postbox on the day to put cards in to keep them safe.

Photos

We would ask that you don't take photos during the ceremony as we want to make sure that the photographers have the chance to get all the photos. We will make sure to share these out for everyone to see afterwards. There will be a chance to take pictures during the signing of the register and then after the ceremony get snap-happy!

Please don't post any photos online until the next day, just so evening guests don't get any spoilers!

After the day, you'll be able to add photos here: https://photos.app.goo.gl/39N6ns72ELYZ6TBz5. We would love everyone to use this to share their photos from the day with us.
